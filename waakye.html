Waakye Recipe

The best waakye

With your beans cooking on fire,you will then add some rice to the beans on fire. After that you add your waakye leaves to give to give the rice and beans that beautiful color.

After the main food is prepared, you will now add the shito and the stew to give it that greate taste. Some people add gari or spaghetti or both. Then voila ton allimangne est pret.

Ingredients

  • Beans
  • Rice
  • Waakye Leaves
  • Salade

Steps

  1. Cook the beans
  2. Wash the rice
  3. Add the rice to the beans
  4. Leave on fire for 45 mins
